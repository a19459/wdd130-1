About Me

Ed Swanson

Greetings everone. I have had many nicknames but most I cannot post here. I am currently working towards my degree in Computer Science, but my end goal is earn a PhD in Digital Forensics.

I am married to a eternal companion, Tammy, and we just celebrated our 30th wedding anniversary. We have two awesome sons and we are hoping for grand kids.

I have been in the IT field for over 42 years and installed the first CD-Rom drive for the CompUAdd - Culebra store I worked at in San Antonio. I currently work for a law enforcement agency in Sacramento, CA as a Digital Forensic Analyst. I am lucky to have a strong faith base, because what I have seen in the course of my job is heartbreaking to say the least.

I became a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Sainta when I was 33. Before that, I was Protestant, but have lived in many places as a dependant of a military family, so I have been to many different churches. I have been to Methodist, Protestant, Baptist, Southern Baptist, Seven Day Adventist, multiple non-denominational churches, Shinto shrines, Buddhist temple, and even a Jewish synagogue. I have friends from many different faiths and non-faith, but I am grateful for my wife and her introducing me the Bo0ok of Mormon.
