Introducing Parental Controls

Help guide how teens use ChatGPT — choose settings that work for your family.

What Parents Can Control

Reduce sensitive content

Limit what content your teen can see.

Quiet hours

Disable ChatGPT during certain times.

Link accounts

Send an invite to your teen, and once accepted, control settings from your parent account.

How It Works

  1. Parent sends invite
  2. Teen accepts and links account
  3. Parent picks settings
  4. Controls activate immediately
